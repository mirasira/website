Miroslav David

I am a PhD student in Cybernetics and Robotics at the Czech Institute of Informatics, Robotics, and Cybernetics (CIIRC) and the Faculty of Electrical Engineering, CTU in Prague.

My research focuses on Human-Robot Collaboration and Human-In-The-Loop Learning. Specifically, I am investigating how to inject human intent into geometric motion planners using Vision-Language Models (VLMs) for industrial applications.

I am supervised by Robert Babuška and co-supervised by Karla Štěpánová.

I hold both Bachelor's and Master's degrees in Cybernetics and Robotics from CTU in Prague.
